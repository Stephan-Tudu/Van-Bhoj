About VAN BHOJ
What is VAN BHOJ? ‚ñº
VAN BHOJ is a tribal foods marketplace that connects authentic tribal cuisine from across India directly to your table. We work with indigenous communities to preserve their culinary heritage while providing fair trade opportunities.
How does VAN BHOJ support tribal communities? ‚ñº
We ensure that 80% of every purchase goes directly to tribal artisans. We also support sustainable farming practices and provide a platform to preserve and celebrate tribal culinary traditions.
Are all products organic? ‚ñº
Yes! We are committed to 100% organic sourcing. All our ingredients are naturally sourced from tribal communities using sustainable and traditional farming methods.
Ordering & Delivery
How do I place an order? ‚ñº
Visit our Menu page, select your desired dishes, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your order.
What is the delivery time? ‚ñº
We typically deliver within 24 hours of order placement. Delivery time may vary based on your location. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.
